Lawn Mowing

Weekly or bi-weekly cuts, edging, and clippings cleanup. Reliable schedule, clean finish.

Learn More

Grass grows fastest in late spring and early summer. If it gets too long between cuts, taking it short all at once stresses the lawn and leaves brown patches. Steady weekly or bi-weekly cuts at the right height keep the grass even, healthy, and green all season — and stop weeds from taking over.

Leaf Cleanup

Full seasonal cleanups — fall and spring. Hauled away, not left in bags on your curb.

Learn More

Fall leaves look nice for a few days, then they get wet and matted on the ground. Once that happens, they block sunlight from your grass and trap moisture against the soil — perfect conditions for mold and dead patches by spring. Clearing them on time keeps your lawn healthy through winter so it comes back green in spring.

Snow Removal

Driveways and walkways cleared within 12 hours of snowfall. Salting available.

Learn More

Snow that sits gets walked on and driven over, which packs it into ice. Once it freezes, it blocks your driveway, makes the walkway slippery, and salt barely touches it anymore. Fresh snow comes off in minutes; frozen snow takes hours. Clearing on time keeps the property safe and saves you from a much bigger job later.

Common questions

Do you offer one-time visits or only seasonal contracts?

Both. We offer one-time cleanups, single visits, and full seasonal plans. Founding customers can lock in 15% off their first full season.

Can I pay per visit instead of a seasonal contract for snow?

Yes — we offer both. Seasonal: a flat rate covering every storm Dec–Mar. From $450 for a small driveway, $600 medium, $800 large. Per-visit: $40 small / $55 medium / $75 large per push. Add $15 for salting. Most customers choose seasonal because GTA winters average 12–18 plowable storms, which works out cheaper per-storm. Per-visit is there if you'd rather only pay when we show up.

What happens if it doesn't snow much in a given winter?

If you're on a seasonal contract, you pay the same flat rate regardless — that's the trade-off for unlimited service if the winter ends up heavy. If you're on per-visit pricing, you only pay when we plow. Mild winters reward per-visit customers; heavy winters reward seasonal customers.

Do you bring your own equipment?

Yes. Mowers, blowers, plows, and salt — we arrive with everything we need.

How fast do you respond after a snowfall?

Residential properties are cleared within 12 hours of snowfall ending. Priority routes are cleared sooner.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes — $5M liability insurance and full WSIB coverage. We can send our certificate of insurance on request.

What's your cancellation policy?

30 days' notice to cancel a seasonal plan. One-time visits can be rescheduled up to 24 hours before.
